Red Velvet Cupcakes

Ingreident List

The ingredients include:

  • 1 3/4 cups (245 g) all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup (30 g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup (240 ml) whole milk (Replaceable with any plant milk you like)
  • 1 tablespoon (15 ml) apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup (115 g) unsalted butter, softened (1 stick)
  • 1 cup (200 g) granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon (15 ml) vegetable or canola oil
  • 1 teaspoon (5 ml) pure vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons (10 ml) red food color

Steps to make the Cupcakes

The steps to make the red velvet delights are:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C). Line a cupcake pan with cupcake liners.
  2. Combine flour, baking powder, salt, and cocoa powder in a bowl. Set aside.
  3. In a mixing bowl or liquid measuring cup, combine milk and vinegar.
  4. Cream butter and sugar creamy and pale on medium-high speed, about 3 to 4 minutes. Then add oil and vanilla; continue beating to combine, about 1 minute. Turn the mixer to low and add the flour mixture in three batches, alternating with milk mixture, beginning and ending with the flour mixture; beat until just combined, about 30 to 45 seconds. Add the food coloring and mix until just combined. Do not overmix.
  5. Divide batter evenly among liners, about 3/4 full each.
  6. Bake for 18 â€“ 22 minutes on the same shelf, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Cool pan on wire racks for 5 minutes, then remove cupcakes from pan, place back on the rack, and cool to room temperature before frosting, about 1 hour.

Oreo Pudding

Ingredient List

The ingredients include:

  • Chocolate Pudding
  • Milk
  • Cool Whip
  • Cream Cheese
  • Oreo Cookies
  • Ganache

Steps to Make the Ingredient

The steps to make this delicious oreo pudding are:

  1. Crush the Oreos in a food processor or blender and set them aside.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the instant pudding and cold milk with a blender until the mixture thickens into a pudding.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat cream cheese until creamy. Add the cream cheese mixture and cool whip to the pudding and mix until combined.
  4. In a saucepan over low heat, melt the heavy cream and chocolate until a thick and creamy ganache forms.
  5. Add the creamy pudding mixture into a 9 by 13 dish and spread evenly. Top the chocolate pudding layer with the crushed Oreos and drizzle with the ganache. (You can also top this dessert with homemade whipped cream instead of chocolate ganache.)
  6. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour (or overnight).
